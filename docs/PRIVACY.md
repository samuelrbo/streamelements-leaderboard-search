# Privacy Policy for StreamElements Leaderboard Search

_Effective Date: March 25, 2026_

## 1. Overview

StreamElements Leaderboard Search ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

## 2. Data Collection ("Website Content")

When you visit a StreamElements leaderboard page, the Extension accesses the **Website Content** (DOM elements) of `streamelements.com`.

- **What we read:** We process the leaderboard table data and the streamer's username from the URL.
- **Purpose:** This access is strictly used to inject the search interface and calculate your current ranking position to display in the sidebar widget.

## 3. Data Storage and Transmission

- **Local Processing:** All data identified as "Website Content" is processed locally within your browser.
- **No External Servers:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data, search queries, or browsing history to external servers.
- **No Third Parties:** Your data is never sold, traded, or shared with third parties.

## 4. Authentication and Cookies

The Extension does not access your StreamElements login credentials, passwords, or session cookies. We only use public API endpoints provided by StreamElements to fetch ranking data based on the public ID of the channel you are visiting.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and updated in our GitHub repository.
